1942

There are times and then there are eras – eras defined by extraordinary circumstances and led by even more extraordinary people. The Iconic Collection of HMT pays a classic tribute to icons who led movements that have changed the course of history or changed lives forever. 1942 is the first edition and a collection tribute to Mohandas Karamchand often known the world over and across generations as Gandhi or the Mahatma as christened by Nobel Laureate Rabindranath Tagore.

Quite a movement. A time immemorial.

Made by women and men of exceptional character and uncommon grit. They make the world of ours better, more responsible, with their words and actions, taking the world in their stride. Some take down empires. Many start a movement. The rare do both.

History is never given. It is made.

Gandhi’s call for the Quit India movement changed not only India’s history forever but brought in an early sunset for the British Empire for which it was claimed, the sun never sets. Led by a principled and fanatical belief in the power of non-violence, and wrapped in moral courage, the rebellion made the oppressed believe that their time had come.
